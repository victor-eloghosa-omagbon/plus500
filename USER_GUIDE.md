## Getting Started

After signing up and verifying your email, log in to access your dashboard. You'll see a sidebar on the left with all your navigation options and an account summary at the top.

Your account starts with a **$0.00 balance**. To begin trading, you'll need to deposit funds first (see [Deposit & Withdraw](#deposit--withdraw)).

---

## Understanding Your Account Summary

At the top of your sidebar, you'll see five key numbers. Here's what each one means:

| Metric | What It Means | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Balance** | Your available cash — money not currently in a trade | $1,000.00 |
| **Equity** | Your total account value right now (Balance + any profit/loss from open trades) | $1,050.00 |
| **Margin** | How much of your money is currently locked in open trades | $200.00 |
| **Free Margin** | How much you can still use to open new trades (Equity minus Margin) | $850.00 |
| **Open P/L** | Your unrealized profit or loss — green means you're up, red means you're down | +$50.00 |

> 💡 **Tip:** Open P/L changes constantly with market prices. It only becomes real profit or loss when you **close** the trade.

## Markets

The **Markets** page shows all available trading instruments with:

- **Live prices** updating in real time
- **Spread** — the small difference between buy and sell price (this is the cost of trading)
- **24h change** — how much the price has moved today (green = up, red = down)

Click on any instrument to view more details or place a trade.

> 💡 **Tip:** Use the star icon to add instruments to your Watchlist for easy access later.

## Placing a Trade

Navigate to **Trade** from the sidebar or click any asset to open the trade panel.

### Step-by-step:

1. **Select an instrument** — Choose from the dropdown (instruments show their full names like "Gold" or "Crude Oil")
2. **Choose Buy or Sell**
   - **Buy** = you think the price will go **up**
   - **Sell** = you think the price will go **down**
3. **Enter your amount** — How much money (in USD) you want to invest in this trade
4. **Set your leverage** — This multiplies your exposure:
   - **1:1** = no leverage (safest)
   - **1:10** = $100 controls $1,000 worth (higher risk, higher potential reward)
   - **1:50** = $100 controls $5,000 worth (very risky for beginners)
5. **Set Stop Loss (optional but recommended)** — The price at which your trade automatically closes to limit your loss
6. **Set Take Profit (optional)** — The price at which your trade automatically closes to lock in your profit
7. **Click Place Order** to execute

> ⚠️ **Important for beginners:** Start with **small amounts** and **low leverage** (1:1 or 1:2) until you understand how price movements affect your P/L.

### Understanding Leverage — A Simple Example:

| You Invest | Leverage | You Control | Price Goes Up 5% | Your Profit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| $100 | 1:1 | $100 | +5% | +$5 |
| $100 | 1:10 | $1,000 | +5% | +$50 |
| $100 | 1:50 | $5,000 | +5% | +$250 |

> ⚠️ Leverage works both ways — if the price drops 5% with 1:50 leverage, you'd lose $250 on a $100 investment.

## Charts

The **Charts** page displays price charts for all instruments in your Watchlist.

- Each chart shows recent price movement with visual trends
- Green trend = price going up, Red trend = price going down
- Click on any chart to go to that instrument's trade page

> 💡 **Tip:** Add instruments to your Watchlist first — only watchlisted items appear on the Charts page.

## Open Positions

This page shows all your **currently active trades**. For each position you'll see:

- **Instrument** — What you're trading
- **Direction** — Buy or Sell
- **Entry Price** — The price when you opened the trade
- **Current Price** — The live market price
- **Amount Invested** — How much you put in
- **Leverage** — Your multiplier
- **P/L** — Your current unrealized profit or loss

You can **close a position** from this page to realize your profit or cut your losses. When you close:
- Your original investment amount **plus** any profit is added back to your balance
- Or your original investment **minus** any loss is returned

---

## Orders

View and manage your **pending orders** — these are trades you've set up that haven't been executed yet (e.g., limit orders waiting for a specific price).

- **Pending** — Waiting to be filled
- **Filled** — Successfully executed (these move to Open Positions)
- **Cancelled** — Orders you've cancelled

## Deposit & Withdraw

### Depositing Funds
1. Go to **Deposit / Withdraw** in the sidebar
2. Select your deposit method (crypto)
3. Follow the instructions to send funds to the provided wallet address
4. Your deposit will be reviewed by an administrator and credited to your balance once approved

### Withdrawing Funds
1. Go to **Deposit / Withdraw**
2. Select **Withdraw**
3. Enter the amount and your withdrawal details
4. Submit your request — an administrator will review and process it

> ⏱ Deposits and withdrawals require admin approval for security purposes.

## Price Alerts

Set up notifications for when an instrument hits a specific price.

### How to create an alert:
1. Go to **Alerts** in the sidebar
2. Choose the instrument (e.g., Gold)
3. Set your condition:
   - **Price goes above** a target price
   - **Price goes below** a target price
4. Enter your target price
5. Save the alert

You'll be notified when the condition is met.

## Settings & Two-Factor Authentication

### Profile Settings
- Update your **display name**
- View your account information

### Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for account security:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Select your **country code** from the dropdown
4. Enter your **mobile phone number**
5. Click **Enable 2FA** — wait 3 seconds for verification
6. Done! Your account is now more secure

## 🎓 Quick Tips for Beginners

1. **Start small** — Don't invest more than you can afford to lose
2. **Use low leverage** — Stick to 1:1 or 1:2 until you're comfortable
3. **Always set a Stop Loss** — This protects you from large unexpected losses
4. **Use your Watchlist** — Track instruments you're interested in before trading them
5. **Check your Open P/L regularly** — Know where your trades stand
6. **Review your history** — Learn from past trades to improve your strategy
7. **Enable 2FA** — Protect your account with two-factor authentication
8. **Don't panic** — Markets go up and down. Stick to your plan.
